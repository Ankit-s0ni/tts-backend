Kal raat main apne dost ke saath long drive par gaya tha. Mausam itna cool tha ki humne gaadi ki windows khol kar slow music chala diya. Highway par traffic bilkul kam tha, isliye drive aur bhi enjoyable ho gayi. Beech mein hum ek small dhaba par rukkar chai aur pakode khaye. Wapas aate waqt hum log future plans ke baare mein baat karte hue bahut hasi-majak kar rahe the. Overall, poora experience bohot hi awesome tha
